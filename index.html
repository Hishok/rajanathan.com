Multi-Factor Equity Premia Study
Systematic Research · Factor Investing · S&P 500 / FTSE 100
Evaluated four published equity factor premia — momentum (UMD), value (HML), quality (RMW), and low-volatility — across a dual-universe dataset. Constructed equal-weight and risk-parity composite portfolios; risk-parity achieved a Sharpe of 0.17 with a diversification ratio of 1.41x. Conducted subsample stability analysis across four decades, rolling Sharpe decomposition, and crisis-period attribution covering the 2009 momentum crash, COVID drawdown, and 2022 rate shock.
